QKD Based Fiber Connect 2024

August 9th - 10th, 2024 | NSUT, New Delhi

This workshop will feature a series of scientific talks and demonstrations, covering both theoretical and experimental aspects of QKD and related technologies. The proposed programme outline is as follows:

  • Quantum Key Distribution (QKD)
  • Hybrid Quantum Systems
  • Quantum Interference and Coherence
  • Nonlinear Quantum Optics
  • Photonic Quantum Communication
  • Quantum Simulations
  • Quantum Entanglement
  • Quantum Light Sources
  • Single-photon Detectors
  • Quantum Repeaters and Relays
  • Quantum Random Number Generator (QRNG)
