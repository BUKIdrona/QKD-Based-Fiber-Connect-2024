QKD Based Fiber Connect 2024

August 9th - 10th, 2024 | NSUT, New Delhi

Speakers

Atul Kumar Gupta

(C-DOT, Delhi)

Title of the Talk (Tentative): Overview of Li-Fi Technology

Short bio: Prof. Anand Srivastava assumed the role of Vice Chancellor at Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), Delhi on November 29, 2023. Prior to his appointment at NSUT, he held the position of Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at IIIT Delhi. In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Prof. Srivastava served as the Director of the IIIT Delhi Incubation Center and held the position of Adjunct Faculty in the Bharti School of Telecom Technology at IIT Delhi. Before joining IIIT Delhi, Prof. Srivastava served as the Dean and Professor in the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi, Himachal Pradesh, India, from January 2012 to November 2014. Prof. Srivastava worked at the Alcatel-Lucent-Bell Labs, India, as a Solution Architect for access and core networks for 2.5 years. Notably, His professional journey also includes a significant tenure of ~19 years at the Center for Development of Telematics (CDOT), a prestigious telecom research center of the Government of India. During his time at CDOT, he held the position of Director and was a Member of the CDOT Board. He led a team of engineers focused on the development of national-level projects in Telecom Security Systems, Network Management System, Intelligent Networks, Operations Support Systems, Access Networks (GPON), and Optical Technology-based products.

Abhijit Mitra

(IIIT Delhi )

Title of the Talk (Tentative): Overview of Li-Fi Technology

Short bio: Abhijit Mitra received his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (2017) under the funding of the British Telecom Fellowship. His experience has been in modeling and network planning for metro and core optical networks. He has been a reviewer in reputed journals like the Journal of Optical Communication and Networking (JOCN), and the Journal of Lightwave Technology (JLT). He has published in major venues like Proceedings of the IEEE, JOCN, JLT, Optical Fiber Communications (OFC) Conference, and the European Conference on Optical Communications (ECOC) with due industry-academic collaborations. Further, he has led funded projects by DST, MIETY, and DRDO in the capacity of PI/Co-PI and functioning as a collaborator in a project funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF), USA. He has been awarded DST Inspire Faculty Fellowship (2017-2022) by DST, British Council (Alumni Awards): Professional Achievement Awards (2019) by British Council, the prestigious Fulbright Post Doctoral Research Fellowship by the United States India Education Foundation (USIEF) and Sparkle-Marie Sk≈Çodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Cofund Fellowship (2022, not availed). Overall he has 10 years of research experience in Transport Optical Networks.
